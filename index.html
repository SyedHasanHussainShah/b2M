Chain Wallet User Guide

Getting Started

To begin using Chain Wallet, you first need to connect your wallet by clicking the "Connect" button in the main screen. You'll be prompted to enter your password (default: 1234).

Sending Transactions

1. Navigate to the "Quick Send" tab
2. Enter the recipient's address (or select from your contacts)
3. Enter the amount to send
4. Select a transaction category (optional)
5. Add a note (optional)
6. Click "Send ETH"

Receiving Funds

1. Click the "Receive" button to display your wallet address
2. Share your address or QR code with the sender
3. Once the transaction is complete, you'll receive a notification

Managing Contacts

1. Click the address book icon next to the recipient field
2. Click the "+" button to add a new contact
3. Enter the contact's name and address
4. Click "Save"

Changing Your Password

1. Go to the "Account" tab
2. Scroll to the "Security" section
3. Click "Change" next to "Change Password"
4. Enter your current password and new password
5. Click "Update Password"

Two-Factor Authentication

1. Go to the "Account" tab
2. Scroll to the "Security" section
3. Toggle the switch next to "Two-Factor Authentication"
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up 2FA
